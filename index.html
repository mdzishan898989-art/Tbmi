Principal's Corner

MD Ahsan Habib

Principal, Technical & Business Management Institute

Dear Students, Faculty Members, and Well-wishers,

It is my privilege to serve as the Principal of Technical & Business Management Institute. Since our establishment in 2004, TBMI has been a beacon of technical and business education in northern Bangladesh. Our mission has always been to nurture skilled professionals who can meet the demands of the modern workforce.

At TBMI, we believe education is not just about textbooks — it's about building character, fostering innovation, and preparing our students to become ethical leaders. Our dedicated faculty members work tirelessly to ensure every student receives personalized attention and practical training.

I invite all prospective students to visit our campus and experience the TBMI difference. Together, we can build a brighter future.

May your journey with us be transformative and rewarding.

MD Ahsan Habib
Principal
