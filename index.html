College History & Tradition

Technical and Business Management Institute (TBMI) was founded in 2004 in Bhawlagonj, Debiganj, Panchagarh with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between technical skills and business knowledge. Starting with a small group of dedicated educators, TBMI quickly became a trusted name for quality technical education in northern Bangladesh.

Over the years, the institute has expanded its programs, introduced modern labs, and maintained a strong focus on practical, career-oriented training. Today, TBMI continues its mission to develop skilled professionals who contribute to the nation's economy.

Our Mission

High quality technical & business education with practical skills, innovation, and professionalism. We strive to produce graduates who are ready for the modern workforce.

Our Vision

Leading technical institute in Bangladesh, producing skilled graduates who will drive innovation and ethical leadership in technology and business sectors.
